Sourcing & Services

An overview of how BOLYMAX supports buyers with product sourcing from China, OEM/ODM projects and end-to-end import handling into the UK.

Core Services

We focus on practical B2B sourcing and trade services, designed around buyers who need a stable partner to manage their China-side supply chain.

  • • Product sourcing from suitable factories in China
  • • Supplier matching based on your brief and target price level
  • • OEM / ODM project coordination
  • • Quality inspection and pre-shipment checks
  • • International logistics and UK import clearance
  • • Local wholesale and distribution support in the UK
Typical use cases
  • Developing a new product range for the UK market
  • Consolidating multiple suppliers into one managed channel
  • Moving from trial orders to stable, repeat shipments

Project Flow

  1. 1. Brief & requirements – product type, target market, quality level, pricing range.
  2. 2. Supplier search – we shortlist suitable factories in China.
  3. 3. Quotations & samples – pricing, lead times and sample confirmation.
  4. 4. Production follow-up – order placement, progress and updates.
  5. 5. Quality checks – inspection arrangements before shipment.
  6. 6. Logistics & import – shipping, customs clearance and delivery.
What we ask from buyers
  • Clear description of the product and application
  • Indicative target price level
  • Estimated order volume and frequency
  • Any special requirements (certifications, packaging, labelling)
