Sourcing & Services

BOLYMAX LTD provides end-to-end support for sourcing from Chinese factories and importing into the UK, from initial product search to delivery.

Core Sourcing Services

Our services are designed to give buyers a clear, structured and reliable route to sourcing from China.

  • • Product sourcing from Chinese manufacturers
  • • Supplier research and vetting
  • • Matching factories to your specifications and price levels
  • • Coordination of quotations, samples and production
Approach

We start from your requirements, not from a fixed catalogue. This allows us to search for suitable factories and product options that best match your needs in terms of quality, price and lead time.

OEM / ODM & Quality

  • • OEM and ODM project support
  • • Coordination of artwork, packaging and branding requirements
  • • Quality inspection and reporting
  • • Follow-up on remedial actions where needed
Focus on quality

We work with factories that understand the expectations of the UK and European markets. Where necessary, we help arrange third-party inspections or additional checks to support your internal quality processes.

Logistics & Import Handling

  • • Coordination of international logistics from China to the UK
  • • Support with customs clearance arrangements
  • • Deliveries to UK warehouses or distribution centres
  • • Optional support with UK wholesale and distribution
Simple, end-to-end process

Rather than dealing with multiple parties, you can work with BOLYMAX as a single point of contact between your business, the factory and the logistics chain.

To discuss a project or explore how BOLYMAX LTD can support your sourcing and import plans, please send us an enquiry with your product requirements.
