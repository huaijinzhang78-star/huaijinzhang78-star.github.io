Who We Are

BOLYMAX LTD is a UK-registered trading company based in London. We connect Chinese manufacturing with the UK and European markets through a straightforward and reliable sourcing process.

Our role is to act as your local UK partner while managing product sourcing, factory communication and coordination across China. We work with importers, wholesalers and retailers who require a stable, long-term sourcing partner.

Key points
  • UK company, subject to UK regulations
  • Chinese factory network with verified suppliers
  • Focus on medium to long-term cooperation

What We Do

We provide end-to-end support for sourcing and importing products from China to the UK.

  • • Product sourcing from Chinese manufacturers
  • • Supplier matching based on your specifications
  • • OEM and ODM project support
  • • Quality inspection and coordination
  • • International logistics and UK import clearance
How it works

You share your brief – product type, target price level, estimated volume and timing. We then identify suitable factories, coordinate quotations and guide you through sampling, ordering and shipment to the UK.
