About BOLYMAX LTD

BOLYMAX LTD is a UK-registered trading company based in London, specialising in connecting Chinese manufacturing with buyers in the UK and Europe.

Our Role

We combine a UK front office with a strong Chinese supply chain network. Our job is to make it easier and more predictable for our clients to source products from China.

For buyers, we act as a local partner in the UK — one that understands product, compliance, logistics and communication with Chinese factories. For factories, we provide structured access to UK and European demand.

We focus on practical, medium-to-long term cooperation instead of one-off deals. This means clear communication, realistic timelines and a consistent approach to quality and documentation.

Company profile
  • UK-registered trading company
  • Head office in London, United Kingdom
  • Network of manufacturing partners across China
  • Focus on consumer and fast-moving products

How We Work with Clients

Each project starts with a clear brief: product type, quality level, target price, estimated volumes and timing. Based on this, we identify suitable factories and coordinate quotations, samples and production.

Throughout the process we remain the main point of contact, ensuring information flows reliably between the buyer and the factory and that key steps such as quality checks and shipping arrangements are properly managed.

Typical cooperation model
  • Brief and requirement discussion
  • Factory search and supplier shortlist
  • Quotations, samples and confirmation
  • Production follow-up and quality checks
  • Logistics, customs clearance and delivery

If you are considering sourcing from China and would like a UK-based partner to help manage the process, we would be pleased to discuss your requirements.
